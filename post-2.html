Course Overview

This course helps students improve their communication skills in spoken and written English. It focuses on grammar, vocabulary, sentence structure, and clarity in expression. It also teaches how to write essays, letters, and reports effectively.

Topics Covered

  • Parts of Speech
  • Sentence Structure
  • Paragraph Writing
  • Essay Writing
  • Formal and Informal Letters
  • Comprehension and Summary

Examples

Example 1: Correct sentence structure – “The students study every evening.”

Example 2: Paragraph Writing – Begin with a topic sentence, develop your idea in the middle sentences, and end with a concluding sentence.

Why It Matters

Good English communication helps you perform better in other subjects, present ideas clearly, and succeed in academic and professional life.
