🎯 Study Tips for First Semester

Sep 10, 2025

Plan a weekly schedule. Set two main goals each day. Use short focused study blocks of 25 minutes. Take a 5 minute break after each block. Review notes weekly.

How to take notes

Write a short heading. Note three main points. Add one example. Keep each entry under 100 words.

Example

Topic: Active and passive voice. Active uses the subject performing the action. Passive uses the subject receiving the action. Example: "She writes notes." vs "Notes are written by her."
